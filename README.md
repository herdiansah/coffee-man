# Coffee Man

Coffee Man is an exciting 2D platformer game built with HTML5 Canvas and Vanilla JavaScript. Guide Coffee Man through a procedurally generated world, collect coffee ingredients, avoid monsters, and master the unique multi-jump mechanic to reach the goal!

## üéÆ Features

*   **Dynamic Multi-Jump System**: Your jump power increases with each consecutive jump while in the air, allowing you to reach greater heights. Landing resets your jump power.
*   **Procedural Level Generation**: Platforms, enemies, and collectibles are generated dynamically, offering a fresh experience each time.
*   **Collectibles**:
    *   **Coffee Beans**: +10 Score
    *   **Milk**: +50 Score
*   **Enemies**: Watch out for patrolling monsters!
*   **Responsive Design**: The game adapts to your browser window size.
*   **Parallax Background**: Immersive scrolling background effects.

## üöÄ How to Play

1.  **Start the Game**: Open `index.html` in your web browser.
2.  **Objective**: Navigate right to reach the goal at the end of the level.
3.  **Survive**: You have 3 lives. Avoid falling off the map or touching monsters.
4.  **Score High**: Collect as many beans and milk cartons as you can!
